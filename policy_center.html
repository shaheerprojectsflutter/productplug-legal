Shipping Policy

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 3–5 business days. Once your order is processed, you will receive a tracking number to monitor your shipment.

Shipping Carriers

We use reliable carriers including USPS, UPS, and FedEx to ensure your order is delivered safely and efficiently.

Delivery Times

  • Express Items: 5–10 business days
  • Standard Items: 2–4 weeks

Delivery times may vary depending on product availability and shipping destination. Standard items may require additional time to source before shipment.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your order and delivery location. Occasionally, promotional codes for free shipping may be offered.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your order is lost or arrives damaged, we provide store credit or a replacement (if the item is in stock). Please note that refunds are not available. Contact our support team promptly to resolve any issues.

Contact Us

For questions regarding your order or shipping, please contact our support team — we're here to help.

Refund & Return Policy

Return Eligibility

  • Returns are accepted within 14 days of the order date.
  • All returns are issued as store credit only (no cash refunds).
  • A receipt or order confirmation email is required for all returns.
  • No returns are accepted without proof of purchase.

Condition Requirements

  • Items must be unused, unopened, and in their original packaging.
  • Items will be inspected upon return; store credit will be issued only after approval.
  • Please allow 3–5 business days for processing after the returned item is received.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Cosmetics and personal care items
  • Food, snacks, and beverages
  • Intimate apparel
  • Seasonal items
  • Electronic accessories
  • Bundled items once opened
  • Clearance or final-sale items

Exchanges

  • Exchanges may be made for an item of equal or lower value.
  • Exchanges follow the same 14-day and condition requirements.

Shipping Costs

  • Customer-preference returns require the customer to pay return shipping.
  • If the issue is on our end (wrong item or damaged item), a $10 credit will be issued toward a future $50 purchase.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Provide photos and your order number.
  • Once verified, you will receive $10 off your next order of $50 or more.

Store Credit

  • Store credit may be used toward any future purchase.
  • Store credit cannot be exchanged for cash.

Product Plug Negotiation Policy

1. Overview

The Product Plug Negotiation Policy allows eligible customers to request a price review when they find the same product offered at a lower price by a competitor. Our goal is to offer the most competitive pricing possible while maintaining product quality and availability.

2. Eligibility Requirements

To request a negotiation or undercut, you must provide:

  • A valid link or proof of the competitor's current price
  • Evidence that the competitor's product is the same model, condition, and configuration (new, used, refurbished, etc.)
  • Proof that the competitor's price is publicly available and active at the time of verification

3. Verification Process

Once a request is submitted, Product Plug will:

  1. Verify the competitor's listed price
  2. Confirm that the product is in stock in our inventory
  3. Determine whether we can negotiate or undercut the competitor's pricing

4. Possible Outcomes

If the item is in stock, we may:

  • Offer to negotiate the price with you, or
  • Undercut the competitor's price if feasible

Product Plug is not obligated to match or beat any price, and all price decisions are made at our sole discretion.

5. Exclusions

The following are not eligible unless explicitly approved:

  • Flash sales, clearance sales, or limited-time promotions
  • Pricing errors or misprints
  • Bundles or multi-item offers
  • Auction-based listings (eBay, OfferUp, Facebook Marketplace, etc.)
  • Non-verifiable prices

Product Plug Holiday Toy Donation Policy

Policy Name: Product Plug Holiday Giving Initiative

Effective Period: November 1 – December 15 (annually)

Overview

During the holiday season, Product Plug encourages customers to support families in need by donating toys through our platform. To make giving easier, Product Plug reduces the cost of donated toys when customers participate.

How It Works

When you purchase toys specifically for donation through the Product Plug app, Product Plug will discount $5 for every $30 you spend on those donation-designated toys.

Example:

  • Spend $30 on toys to donate → You pay $25
  • Spend $60 on toys to donate → You pay $50
  • Spend $90 on toys to donate → You pay $75

The discount applies only to toys purchased for donation, not regular personal purchases.

Eligibility

  • Applies to all toys purchased through Product Plug between November 1 and December 15 that are marked and confirmed as donation items at checkout
  • Only completed, non-refunded donation purchases qualify

Distribution of Donated Toys

All donated toys will be:

  • Purchased brand new
  • Handled directly by Product Plug
  • Delivered to verified community organizations, shelters, or holiday charity programs serving families in need

Program Limits & Modifications

Product Plug may make adjustments to discount amounts, modify program details, or end the initiative early if necessary for fairness and sustainability. Any changes will be communicated promptly.
