Privacy Policy

Product Plug

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Product Plug Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use the Product Plug mobile application and related services ("Product Plug" or "the App").

By using Product Plug, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

A. Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Password or login credentials (secured & encrypted)
  • Uploaded images (product photos, listings, profile images, etc.)

Apple / Google Sign-In

If you sign in using Apple or Google, we receive:

  • Name (if shared by the provider)
  • Email address
  • User ID from the provider

We do not receive your Apple/Google password.

B. Payment Information

Payments are processed by Stripe.

Stripe may collect:

  • Cardholder name
  • Card number
  • Billing information

We do not store or have access to full credit card numbers. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all transactions.

C. Automatically Collected Information

Through Firebase and similar tools, we automatically collect:

  • Device type
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Analytics data (screens viewed, actions taken)
  • Crash logs
  • Cookies and tracking data
  • Push notification tokens

This information helps improve performance, analytics, security, and user experience.

D. No GPS Location Collected

Product Plug does not collect real-time GPS location. We only collect addresses you manually provide for shipping purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and deliver orders
  • Ship items through UPS, FedEx, and USPS
  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud and protect user safety
  • Improve the App and user experience
  • Analyze usage through Firebase Analytics
  • Authenticate logins through Firebase Authentication
  • Maintain security and troubleshoot through Firebase Crashlytics

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to operate Product Plug:

  • Stripe – payment processing
  • Firebase (Google)
    • Authentication
    • Firestore or Realtime Database
    • Analytics
    • Crashlytics
  • Cloudinary – storage and hosting of uploaded photos and product images
  • Shipping Carriers
    • UPS
    • FedEx
    • USPS

Each third party has its own Privacy Policy governing how they handle data.

5. How We Share Information

We share information only as necessary:

With Shipping Carriers

To deliver purchased products:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Contact information

With Stripe

To process secure payments.

With Firebase

For account management, analytics, crash reporting, and database functions.

With Cloudinary

For storing and serving images uploaded by users.

We do NOT:

  • Sell personal information
  • Share personal information with advertisers
  • Use Meta Pixel, Amazon Ads, or similar ad trackers

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and tracking data to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember cart items
  • Improve app functionality
  • Measure performance
  • Analyze how users interact with the App

These tools are typical for e-commerce apps and are not used to track you across unrelated websites.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption
  • Secure servers
  • Limited internal access
  • Firebase authentication protections

No system is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data as best as possible.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to:

  • Provide services
  • Maintain order history
  • Meet legal, tax, or accounting requirements
  • Support your account

You may request data deletion at any time (details below).

9. Your Privacy Rights

If you are a U.S. resident, certain states (including California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and Utah) grant the following rights:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out of data sharing (we do not sell or share your data)

To make a privacy request, contact: officialproductplug@gmail.com

10. Children's Privacy

Product Plug is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided information, contact us for removal.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or update the "Effective Date" above.
